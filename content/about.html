About Matt Culhane

Matt Culhane is a Consultant on People Related topics, an LCOP certified coach and a member of the International Coaching Federation (ICF). He also works with Guttman Development Strategies, Inc.

Matt has over 25 years of experience as a Human Resources Leader at McKinsey & Company, American Express, Novartis, GlaxoSmithKline, and Haleon Consumer Health. That work focused on talent management, strategic workforce planning, leadership development and organizational effectiveness. His background also includes deep expertise in Change Management and Transformation having managed organizations through enormous joint ventures, divestments and demergers. Matt has led and operated globally, working and traveling around the world and living and working in Switzerland from 2014 to 2016.

Throughout his career, he has coached and mentored numerous executives and improved many cross functional executive leadership teams. He is known for his thought leadership, authenticity, candor, optimism, and servant-based leadership, which has helped him deliver on his personal purpose of helping teams and people grow, develop and perform better.
